## 🎫 How to book a ticket

The application uses a logical flow to help you find and reserve seats. Follow these steps for your first booking:

### Search for trips
Open the application from your desktop. The main screen displays a search form. Enter your departure city and your destination. Select your travel date using the calendar tool. Click the "Search" button to see available buses.

### Select a seat
The software shows a list of buses for your selected date. Review the departure times and the ticket prices. Click on a bus to see the layout of the seats. The display shows vacant seats and occupied seats. Click on the seat you prefer to select it.

### Confirm the booking
Once you choose a seat, the application prompts you for passenger details. Enter names and contact information as requested. Review your selection on the summary screen. Click the "Confirm" button to finalize your reservation. The software creates a digital ticket for your records.

## 📁 Managing your reservations

You can view your past and current bookings within the "My Tickets" section of the main menu. This area lists every reservation you made through the software. You can save your ticket as a PDF file to print or show on your phone. If you need to cancel a booking, select the ticket and choose the "Cancel" option. The application updates your status and allows you to search for new trips.

## 🛡️ Privacy and data security

This application stores your passenger information locally on your computer. Your ticket history remains private and accessible only to you through this application. We do not transmit sensitive personal data to external servers beyond what is required to verify your bus route information. You maintain control over your data through the settings menu. You can delete your history at any time by selecting "Clear Cache" in the user options.

## 🔍 Frequently asked questions

### Do I need to update the software?
The application checks for updates when you launch it. You will see a notification if a new version exists. Follow the prompt to download the latest features and security fixes.

### Can I book for multiple people?
Yes. When you reach the passenger details screen, you can add more than one person to your request. The seat selection screen allows you to click multiple empty seats.

### Where is my digital ticket saved?
The program saves tickets in the "Documents" folder on your computer under a sub-folder named "BusTickets". You can change this path in the settings menu if you prefer to save your files elsewhere.

### What if the application freezes?
Close the program and restart your computer. If the problem continues, uninstall the application from your Windows settings and perform a fresh download from the release link. Your saved ticket files remain safe in your documents folder during this process.

### Does the application work without the internet?
You need an internet connection to see new bus schedules and confirm reservations. The application works offline only if you want to view tickets you already saved to your machine.
